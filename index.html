ABOUT ME

profession, interests and skills

About My Profession

I am a UI Developer with 7 years of experience and a moderate amount of back-end experience. I don’t believe in the term "Full Stack Developer", due to the vast coding languages out there, and the rate technology changes makes it impossible to be an expert in everything. I do however believe that one can use knowledge and understanding of basic languages to adapt and accomplish unique project requirements and client demands as a result.

Every project I was ever involved in forced me to learn something new, my curiosity for learning new things and constantly growing is crucial. I believe that hard work always pays off, and being part of the development world I have learned the importance of staying calm :) Being patient and positive to know that every problem has a solution makes any job an effective job.

I have complete experience from start to end phases in agency and corporate projects. This includes planning, strategies, presentations, client services, UI and UX layout, design, time management and quality control. This knowledge and experience gives one a full understanding of every required deliverable in today’s competitive development market.

I work fast and research beforehand if its unfamiliar territory, always dedicated to deliver on time. I don’t believe in missing deadlines, and give all my projects the motivation and perseverance it needs for completion. I am a good listener, able to work in stressful environments; there is nothing more satisfying than solving a problem before it becomes an actual problem, and delivering noticeable results.

When working in teams I found that the most important ingredient for a project's success is communication, for every member to know their role and deadline. Sharing ideas, techniques and suggestions in any team is important for a smooth delivery. I am always willing and approachable when it comes to unfamiliar territory in languages and frameworks. Expanding my knowledge and skills is crucial for career development and satisfaction.

Future Endeavors & Interests
